Karlo is a youth windsurfer from Croatia, Kukuljanovo.

His sailing carrer started over the sumer of 2018 in Optimist.

His first steps were on Optimist and then at the end of 2020 switched to ILCA 4.

His second year was marked with winning the 2022 U16 Men category on Croatian National Championship.

Whilst sailing on ILCA he got in footstraps of Techno 293. Giving him early expirience of windsurfing.

After his last European championship in Izola 2024 he switched to IQ Foil and marking his first year of Windsurfing carrer with the title of Croatian national champion.

Fast forward to August of 2025 he participaded in his first IFCA European championship securing 5th position.

Optimist - the beginning

Over the summer of 2018 he started sailing on optimist. It was Yaht club Galeb that gave him his first steps in sailing along side his coach Walter Kolić.

After learning the basics over the summer he had a lots of trainings during the winter and spring. Bringing him in his first competitive season in 2019. he started attending some local races. On the sailing competiotion Crna Ovca 2019. he secured his first ever race win (in the U12 male category).

Later that year he secured multiple podium finishes and a few more race wins. Ending his debut with a 3rd place in the Primorsko-Goranska County championship in the U12 male and 5th overall

2020

Sailing (competitive) for his 2nd year he had begun participating on international races, racing in both Lago di Garda in both Campione and Riva del Garda as well as participating in Monaco Optimist Team Race 2020.

During 2020 he achieved many race wins and podium finishes in both overall and U12 category.

His second year in competitive sailing was closed with 3rd place overall and 1st place in U12 Male category for Primorsko-Goranska County championship marking his first championship win.

ILCA 4-2021

During COVID Karlo switched to ILCA 4 class. Particiating on many EuropaCup's he gained a lot of expirience for the future years.

Making his debut in this class he had won many local races finishing in 2nd position in Primorsko-Goranska County Chapionship and winning U16 category.

2022

Going into 2022 he participade in the Croatian National Championship in ILCA 4, after 4 days of racing he had won the National Championship making him for the first time a national champion. He went on and won most of local races, ending his second year in this class he also won County's championship in the overall category.

2023

His 3rd year racing in ILCA 4 was a bit of downwards trajectory, not winning the National Championship (3rd) and placing 2nd overall in County's championship weren't his expectations. Mostly affected by loss of his cat (on the very last day of 8th grade) he gave his best which wasn't enough for already high bar set by himself.

2023 from his eyes could not be escribed as an achivement.
Althoguht him winning a lot of races and leading most of the year in he County championship he lost out in last couple of races held in strong wind, and him making a podium finnish on National Championship he could not be happy with the final result.

IQ Foil-2024

He started windsurfing trougth 2023, with more intesified trainings during the fall his first steps were on Techno 293, despite him not placing high places on Techno on Nationals' he kept moving forward. With begnning of 2024 he switched to a foil board and kept on training.
After suffering yet another loss on ILCA 4 with bad performance on 2024 ILCA 4 Europeans' (and volunteers mishandling trailers and "accidentally" breaking his) he switched to IQ Foil.

Still under his fathers training he debuted and won the IQ Foil U17 category on National Championship of 2024 making him a 2 time National champion in pre-olympic class.
Aside him racing IQ Foil he also participated in Foil slalom categories.

2025

His most succesfull year, marking his second year of IQ Foil he participated in IFCA European Championship placing 5th.
Mostly due to lack of expirience and him running a bigger 8.0 sail rather than standarded 7.0 sail for IQ Foil and having an 800 front wing there were just no chances of placing higher.

Later that year he had won yet another Croatian National Championship making him a 3x Croatian Champion in pre-oplympic class.

2026

This year he had not yet participated in such races but very soon he might make a worthy result.
(Karlo Ugrin debuted one again in ILCA 4 in March placing 15th in one race whilst sailing on pre historic equipment, but that equipment proved who is in charge bringing his race to a fast end.)
